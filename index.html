Compound Interest Calculator

Compound interest is interest earned from an initial deposit and any previous interest.

A deposit is the amount of money in your bank account.

The interest rate is a proportion of money the bank pays you for keeping money in your account.

Contributions are money you put in an account regularly.

The number of investment years is how many years you plan to keep the money in your account.

*This calculator is for compounding annually.
